ForeVision Digital

WordPress‑powered platform for digital music distribution and caller‑tune services—built for speed, privacy, and artist control.

Plans & Pricing

  • Pro: ₹498/song — Global stores, Content ID, 90% royalties.
  • CRBT+: ₹699/song — Pro + Jio/Airtel/BSNL caller tunes.
  • CRBT: ₹499/song — Distribution + caller tunes.
  • Social: ₹299/song — TikTok, YT Shorts, 85–90% royalties.

Workflow

  1. Sign up / Log in
  2. Select plan & Click “Upload Now”
  3. Fill metadata & artwork
  4. Choose platforms / subdomain
  5. Pay, receive confirmation, live in 3–4 days

Tech & Constraints

  • WordPress core, custom theme
  • Static‑first HTML/CSS, minimal JS
  • No analytics or trackers
  • Responsive, mobile‑first design
