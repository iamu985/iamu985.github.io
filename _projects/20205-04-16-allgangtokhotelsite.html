AllGangtokHotels.com

A simple, static-driven directory of 634 hotels, 36 homestays, and 29 apartments in Gangtok—built to help travelers find stays without distractions.

Interface & Navigation

  • Language selector with flags; currency switcher supporting 25+ currencies.
  • Check‑in/check‑out date picker; guest count controls.
  • “Where to stay” summary with counts and “Show map” toggle.
  • Filters: star rating, accommodation type, themes, proximity, sights.

Listings

Each entry displays an image gallery, location snippet, “Read more” summary, price per night, “RESERVE” CTA, and rating badge.{index=5}

Pagination and “Top 5★”, “Top 4★” sections guide discovery.

Subdomain structure for individual hotel pages—each site lives at `.allgangtokhotels.com`.

Extras & Footnotes

  • “Recommended hotels” carousel with exceptional scoring.
  • Footer: newsletter signup, “For travellers” and “Company” nav, legal links, payment logos.
  • No trackers; no analytics—privacy by default.

Stack & Constraints

  • Static-first HTML/CSS with progressive enhancement.
  • Responsive design—mobile to desktop.
  • Hand‑crafted templates; no CMS overhead.
  • Minimal JavaScript for filters and date controls.

Notes

  • Simplicity beats bloated platforms.
  • Local‑first UX—maps and filters should load fast, offline‑friendly where possible.
  • Every feature stripped to its essence: select, filter, reserve.
